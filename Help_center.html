How to Order

Step 1: Tap the menu icon (three lines) at the top right corner of the screen.

Step 2: From the menu, tap on Address Book and fill in your delivery address.

Step 3: Go back to the menu and tap Sign Up to create an account.

Step 4: Browse the products and tap on the one you want to personalize.

Step 5: If available, tap the frame area to upload a photo or type a custom message.

Step 6: Once you're done customizing, tap the Add to Cart button. You’ll be taken to your cart page.

Step 7: On the cart page, tap the Checkout button at the bottom.

Step 8: Review your order, scroll down, and choose your payment method (Debit/Credit card or PayPal).

Step 9: If paying by card, please make sure:

  • Your card has enough available funds.
  • Online purchases are enabled in your banking app.

Then, fill in your card details to complete the payment.

Step 10: Confirm your order and wait for confirmation.
