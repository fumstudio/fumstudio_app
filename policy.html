Our Policies

Return Policy for Customized Items

Customized items can only be returned if they were not customized according to your specifications as entered on our website.

We do not accept returns for customized items when the customization was performed exactly as requested by the customer.

If we made an error in the customization process, we will gladly accept the return and provide a refund or replacement.

Delivery Guarantee

If you don't receive your order within 30 days, we offer a money-back guarantee.

This guarantee applies to all orders, including customized items, provided all order information was entered correctly.

Shipping Information

We partner with PUDO for reliable shipping:

Standard shipping time is 7-8 business days.

Please note that shipping times may vary slightly depending on your location and any customs processing for international orders.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our policies or need to initiate a return, please contact our customer service team.
