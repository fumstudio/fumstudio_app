Shipping & Returns Policy

Shipping Information

+

Your orders will be delivered within 1-8 business days.

Due to temporary shortages in shipping resources, your items may arrive in separate packages on different days. We appreciate your understanding and patience.

You will receive tracking information via email as soon as your order is processed and shipped.

International Shipping Details +

International shipping typically takes 10-20 business days, depending on the destination. Customs fees and import duties are the responsibility of the recipient.

Returns Policy

+

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for most items in their original condition.

Standard Returns Process

  • Initiate returns through your account page or contact customer service
  • Return shipping is free for defective or incorrect items
  • Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days after we receive the return
  • Original shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error

Customized Items Returns

+

Customized Items Return Eligibility

Important: Returns for customized items are handled differently than standard products.

  • Customized items are only eligible for returns if there is a manufacturing defect or error on our part.
  • If you customized an item in a way that differs from our available options, or created a design we didn't offer, this is considered a non-standard customization.
  • Items customized according to your personal specifications (beyond our standard options) cannot be returned or exchanged, as they are made specifically for you.

How Returns Work for Customized Items

  • If the item was customized exactly as selected from our available options and has a defect, we will replace it at no cost
  • If you provided custom specifications beyond our standard options, returns are generally not accepted unless the item is defective
  • For personalized items (with names, dates, etc.), returns are only accepted if we made an error in the personalization
  • Refunds for eligible customized item returns may exclude customization fees
Examples of Non-Returnable Customizations +
  • Items with custom text not from our standard list
  • Products with unique color combinations we don't normally offer
  • Items sized outside our standard sizing chart
  • Products with custom artwork you provided
  • Any modification that requires special manufacturing

Exchange Policy

+

We offer exchanges for standard items within 30 days of delivery. Exchanged items must be unused and in original packaging.

Note: Customized items cannot be exchanged unless there is a manufacturing defect or error on our part.
